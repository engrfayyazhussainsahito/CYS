# Lesson 15.5 — Secure by Design, Cyber Resilience, and Future-Ready Programs

## Purpose
Synthesize the full course into a future-ready security program built around secure defaults, secure engineering, cyber resilience, recovery testing, governance evidence, and technology evaluation.

- CISA Secure by Design and Secure by Demand guidance

- NIST SP 800-160 Vol. 2 Rev. 1 Developing Cyber-Resilient Systems
- NIST SP 800-184 Guide for Cybersecurity Event Recovery
- NIST CSF 2.0
- NIST SP 800-34 Rev. 1 contingency planning
- CISA Cross-Sector Cybersecurity Performance Goals
