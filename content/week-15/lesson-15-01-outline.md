## Safety constraints
The lesson is defensive and governance-focused. It avoids jailbreak payloads, model-abuse methods, exploit walkthroughs, real-system testing, model extraction, offensive automation, and unsafe prompt examples. The lab is local, fictional, and performs no network/model calls.
