# Lesson 1.3 — Threats, Vulnerabilities, Exploits, and Risk

This lesson teaches the central risk vocabulary used throughout cybersecurity: threat, threat source, threat event, vulnerability, weakness, exposure, exploit, exploitability, likelihood, impact, inherent risk, residual risk, risk appetite, CVE, CWE, CVSS, NVD, and CISA KEV.

Students build a fictional risk register for the CampusOne university portal and learn why vulnerability severity is not identical to organizational risk.
