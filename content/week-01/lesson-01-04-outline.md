# Lesson 1.4 — Threat Modeling from First Principles

## Purpose
Teach students to create a beginner-safe threat model from first principles, then develop graduate-level reasoning around architecture, trust boundaries, STRIDE, abuse cases, mitigations, residual risk, and uncertainty.

## Core Deliverable
A threat model for a fictional university password-reset flow, including scope, assets, data-flow diagram, trust boundaries, STRIDE analysis, mitigations, assumptions, and open questions.

## Safety Boundary
No public scanning, exploitation, probing, credential testing, or real target analysis. All exercises use fictional systems and local documents only.
