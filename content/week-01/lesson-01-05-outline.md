# Lesson 1.5 — Ethics, Law, and Responsible Disclosure

This lesson teaches authorization, consent, scope, cybersecurity ethics, legal caution, vulnerability disclosure policy, coordinated vulnerability disclosure, safe harbor, security.txt, evidence minimization, redaction, disclosure timelines, research ethics, and a fictional disclosure-kit lab.

Safety scope: all activities are local, fictional, and defensive. Students must not test real systems without written authorization.
