# Lesson 12.4 — Containment, Eradication, and Recovery Runbooks

## Purpose
Teach students how to design defensive incident-response runbooks that stop active harm, preserve necessary evidence, remove root cause, restore trusted service, and document business-risk decisions.

## Research Basis
- NIST SP 800-61 Rev. 3 — Incident Response Recommendations and Considerations for Cybersecurity Risk Management.
- NIST CSF 2.0 — Respond and Recover functions and outcomes.
- CISA Cybersecurity Incident and Vulnerability Response Playbooks.
- NIST SP 800-184 — Guide for Cybersecurity Event Recovery.
- NIST SP 800-86 — Forensic techniques integrated into incident response.
- NIST SP 800-84 — Test, training, and exercise programs.
- CISA/MS-ISAC Ransomware Guide.
