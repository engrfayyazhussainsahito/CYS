# Lesson 12.3: Host, Memory, Network, and Cloud Evidence

## Purpose
Teach defensive responders how to select and preserve host, memory, network, cloud, SaaS, identity, and container evidence without over-collecting, destroying volatile artifacts, or overstating conclusions.

## Research basis
- NIST SP 800-86: collection, examination, analysis, reporting for computer/network forensics in incident response.
- NIST IR 8354: limits of digital investigation techniques and need for tested methods.
- NIST IR 8006: cloud forensic science challenges across architecture, collection, analysis, role management, legal, and standards dimensions.
- NIST SP 800-92: log management planning, collection, storage, analysis, and retention.
- NIST SP 800-61 Rev. 3: current incident response alignment with CSF 2.0.
- AWS Security Incident Response Guide and AWS log-source guidance.
- Microsoft Azure Monitor Activity Log and Microsoft Sentinel Entra ID connector documentation.
- Google Cloud Audit Logs and VPC Flow Logs documentation.
