# Week 12 Overview — Incident Response & Forensics

## Research basis
- NIST SP 800-61 Rev. 3: current NIST incident response guidance, April 2025, supersedes Rev. 2.
- NIST CSF 2.0: Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover.
- CISA Cybersecurity Incident and Vulnerability Response Playbooks: standardized response procedures.
- NIST SP 800-86: integrating forensic techniques into incident response from an IT perspective.
- NIST SP 800-184: cybersecurity event recovery planning and improvement.
- NIST SP 800-84: test, training, and exercise programs.
