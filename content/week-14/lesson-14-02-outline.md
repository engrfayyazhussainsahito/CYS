# Lesson 14.2 — Risk Management, Controls, and Assurance

## Purpose
Teach cybersecurity risk management as a decision discipline that links business objectives, risk scenarios, control selection, evidence, assurance, residual risk, and governance decisions.

## Research basis
- NIST SP 800-30 Rev. 1 — Guide for Conducting Risk Assessments
- NIST SP 800-37 Rev. 2 — Risk Management Framework
- NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 — Security and Privacy Controls
- NIST SP 800-53A Rev. 5 — Assessing Security and Privacy Controls
- NISTIR 8286 Rev. 1 — Integrating Cybersecurity and Enterprise Risk Management
- NIST SP 800-137 — Information Security Continuous Monitoring
- NIST SP 800-55 Vol. 1 — Measurement Guide for Information Security
- CISA Cross-Sector Cybersecurity Performance Goals 2.0
