# Lesson 14.1: Cybersecurity Governance from First Principles

## Research basis
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0: Govern function, Profiles, Tiers, roles/responsibilities, oversight, risk-management strategy, policy, and supply-chain risk management.
- NIST SP 800-37 Rev. 2: Risk Management Framework and continuous monitoring.
- NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5: security and privacy control catalog, tailoring, and assessment context.
- NIST SP 800-55 Vol. 1: information-security performance measurement.
- NIST Privacy Framework: privacy risk governance and enterprise risk-management alignment.
- CISA Cybersecurity Performance Goals 2.0: high-impact baseline practices and governance expectations.
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022: information security management system concept.

The lab performs no network, cloud, legal, regulatory, scanning, authentication, exploitation, or real organizational assessment activity. It uses fictional local JSON data only.
