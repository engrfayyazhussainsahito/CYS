## Summary
DevSecOps pipelines and Infrastructure as Code from first principles: CI/CD, runners, secrets, release gates, IaC plans, policy as code, drift, provenance, artifact integrity, monitoring, rollback, and safe local review labs.

## Safe Lab
Local-only review of YAML and Terraform-style files using a Python script. No real cloud accounts, no scanning public targets, and no Terraform apply.
