# Lesson 16.5 — Final Portfolio, Defense, and Career Roadmap

## Purpose
Close the capstone by converting all prior artifacts into a defensible, sanitized portfolio; a concise final defense; and a role-aware professional development roadmap.

## Research Basis
- NIST CSF 2.0 for outcome organization.
- NIST SP 800-53A Rev. 5 for assessment evidence.
- NIST SP 800-55 Volumes 1 and 2 for measurement and measurement programs.
- NIST SP 800-181 Rev. 1 and current NICE Framework components for task, knowledge, skill, and work-role language.
- CISA Cross-Sector Cybersecurity Performance Goals for baseline measurable practices.
- CISA NICCS NICE Framework tool for career/workforce vocabulary.

## Safe Lab
The lab reads a fictional JSON manifest, checks required sections, validates evidence references, flags unsupported claims, checks defense topics, reviews career targets, and writes a readiness report. It performs no network activity or real assessment.
