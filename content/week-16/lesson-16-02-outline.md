# Lesson 16.2 — Enterprise Security Assessment and Risk Register

## Purpose
Convert the capstone charter into a decision-ready enterprise assessment package and risk register.

## Research basis
- NIST SP 800-30 Rev. 1 — risk assessment guidance.
- NIST SP 800-37 Rev. 2 — RMF lifecycle.
- NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 — security and privacy control catalog.
- NIST SP 800-53A Rev. 5 — control assessment methodology and evidence procedures.
- NIST IR 8286 Rev. 1 — cybersecurity risk registers and enterprise risk management.
- NIST SP 800-55 Vol. 1/2 — information security measurement selection and program design.
- CISA Cross-Sector Cybersecurity Performance Goals — practical baseline practices.
