# Lesson 16.4 — Governance, Privacy, Legal, and Third-Party Executive Brief

## Research basis
- NIST CSF 2.0 for governance, enterprise risk, and executive decision framing.
- NIST SP 800-161 Rev. 1 Update 1 for cybersecurity supply-chain risk management.
- NIST SP 800-53A for evidence and control assessment discipline.
- NIST Privacy Framework and GDPR Articles 33–34 for privacy-escalation concepts.
- SEC cybersecurity-disclosure staff guidance for material-incident disclosure caution.
- CISA CPGs and CIRCIA context for baseline and reporting escalation awareness.

## Safety boundary
The lesson provides executive decision-support structure only. It does not provide legal advice, determine breach notification duties, make securities-disclosure judgments, contact vendors, or assess real organizations.
