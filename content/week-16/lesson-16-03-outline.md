# Lesson 16.3 — Detection, Incident Response, and Forensic Evidence Drill

## Purpose
Turn fictional capstone telemetry into a defensible detection, incident-response, and forensic evidence packet.

## Research basis
- NIST SP 800-61 Rev. 3 for current incident-response framing and CSF 2.0 alignment.
- NIST SP 800-86 for collection, examination, analysis, and reporting in forensic techniques for incident response.
- NIST SP 800-92 for enterprise log-management discipline.
- NIST IR 8387 for digital evidence preservation considerations.
- CISA federal cybersecurity incident and vulnerability response playbooks for standardized identify, coordinate, remediate, recover, and track procedures.
- MITRE ATT&CK for adversary-behavior and detection vocabulary.
- NIST SP 800-84 for test, training, and exercise program design.

## Lab
Safe local Python script that consumes a fictional JSON package and produces triage records, timeline, declaration recommendation, response constraints, and recommended next actions. The lab performs no network activity or real investigation.
