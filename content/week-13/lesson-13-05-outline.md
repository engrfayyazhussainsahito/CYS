# Lesson 13.5: Reporting, Remediation, Retesting, and Purple-Team Handoff

## Research spine
- NIST SP 800-115: assessment planning, conduct, analysis, reporting, and mitigation strategy.
- NIST SP 800-40 Rev. 4: enterprise patch management as preventive maintenance.
- FIRST CVSS v4.0: severity communication.
- FIRST EPSS: probability-oriented exploit likelihood signal.
- CISA KEV and SSVC: known exploited vulnerabilities and stakeholder-specific response decisions.
- MITRE ATT&CK: defender vocabulary and detection/purple-team handoff, with anti-overclaiming guidance.
- OWASP WSTG: structured application-security testing context.
