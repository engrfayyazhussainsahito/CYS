# Lesson 5.4 — Sanitizers, Fuzzing, and Bug Triage

## Purpose
Teach defensive sanitizer use, local fuzzing, and structured bug triage from first principles.

## Major sections
Sanitizers, instrumentation, AddressSanitizer, UBSan, TSan, LeakSanitizer, fuzz targets, harnesses, corpus, mutation, coverage guidance, reproduction, minimization, deduplication, root-cause analysis, severity, priority, regression testing, CI/CD integration, and safe local lab.

## Safety
All examples are local-only toy programs. The lesson explicitly prohibits fuzzing or stressing systems without written authorization.
