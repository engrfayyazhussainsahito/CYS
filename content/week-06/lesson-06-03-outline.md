# Lesson 6.3 — Injection, XSS, and Secure Output

## Summary
Injection from first principles, SQL injection concepts, XSS, sources and sinks, output contexts, parameterized queries, context-aware output encoding, sanitization, CSP as defense in depth, detection, case studies, and safe local-only labs.

## Safety
All examples use toy strings, local SQLite databases, and localhost-only rendering. No real websites, APIs, accounts, or third-party systems are tested.
