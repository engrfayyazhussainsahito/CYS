# Lesson 11.5 — Metrics, Automation, and SOC Maturity

## Purpose
Teach students how to measure SOC effectiveness, choose safe automation candidates, and turn security-operations evidence into a maturity roadmap.

## Research anchors
- NIST SP 800-55 Volume 1 — selecting and evaluating information-security measures.
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0 — outcomes, Profiles, Tiers, and continuous improvement.
- CISA Cybersecurity Performance Goals 2.0 — measurable baseline cybersecurity practices.
- CISA Zero Trust Maturity Model 2.0 — visibility/analytics, automation/orchestration, and governance.
- NIST SP 800-137 — information-security continuous monitoring.
- MITRE ATT&CK data sources and ATT&CK data/tools — coverage and automation vocabulary.

## Lab
Safe local CSV + Python script. No network activity, no real logs, no offensive behavior. Students calculate alert metrics, rule health, automation candidates, and a simple maturity assessment.
