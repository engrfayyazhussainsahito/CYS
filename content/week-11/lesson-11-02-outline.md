Teach students how security telemetry becomes usable evidence before it becomes an alert or incident.

10. Schema and normalization
11. Timestamps, ordering, and time zones
12. Enrichment and context

14. Query thinking before query syntax
15. Practical examples
16. Tools, roles, and ownership
17. Research perspective
18. Common misconceptions
19. Summary
20. Glossary
21. Self-assessment
22. Safe local practice lab
23. Further reading

## Lab
Local fictional JSONL + Python normalizer. No real telemetry collection, no external systems, no attack activity.
