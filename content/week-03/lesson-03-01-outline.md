# Lesson 3.1 — Cryptography from First Principles

## Purpose
Teach cryptography from zero knowledge through core security goals and safe engineering concepts.

## Major Concepts
- Confidentiality, integrity, authenticity, non-repudiation, key establishment
- Encoding vs hashing vs encryption vs MAC vs digital signature
- Plaintext, ciphertext, algorithms, keys, nonces, salts, tags, certificates
- Symmetric cryptography, public-key cryptography, hashes, HMAC, digital signatures
- TLS, certificate validation, key management, crypto agility, post-quantum awareness

## Lab
Local cryptographic building blocks lab using local files, Python hashing, HMAC, and secure token generation.

## Safety Boundary
No unauthorized interception, decryption, scanning, or testing of third-party systems.
