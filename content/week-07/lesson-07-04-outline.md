# Lesson 7.4 — Traffic Analysis, IDS/IPS, and Detection

## Purpose
Teach traffic analysis, IDS, IPS, telemetry, alerts, detection engineering, tuning, and safe local analysis from first principles.

## Major Concepts
- Packets, flows, logs, metadata
- IDS vs IPS
- Sensor placement
- Detection methods
- False positives and false negatives
- Precision and recall
- Zeek, Suricata, Snort-style rules
- Safe fictional log lab

## Safety
Only local, fictional, instructor-provided, or explicitly authorized traffic and logs are used.
