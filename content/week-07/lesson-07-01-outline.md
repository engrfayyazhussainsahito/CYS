# Lesson 7.1 — Network Security from First Principles

Covers hosts, packets, addresses, subnets, CIDR, ports, sockets, TCP, UDP, DNS, routing, NAT, firewalls, segmentation, IDS/IPS, VPNs, telemetry, safe local packet inspection, and defensive network-flow review.

Safety: all examples are limited to localhost, the learner's own machine, or explicitly authorized lab systems.
