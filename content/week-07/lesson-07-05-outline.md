# Lesson 7.5 Outline — VPNs, Wireless, and Secure Network Design

## Core Topics
- VPN concepts and tunnel design
- Remote-access vs site-to-site VPN
- Split tunnel vs full tunnel
- IPsec, TLS VPN, and WireGuard-style concepts
- Wi-Fi access points, SSIDs, WPA2/WPA3
- 802.1X, EAP, RADIUS, enterprise authentication
- Guest Wi-Fi and IoT segmentation
- Secure network-zone design
- Least privilege, deny by default, monitoring, resilience
- Safe fictional network design lab

## Safety
No unauthorized wireless testing, capture, impersonation, jamming, VPN testing, or probing. Use only fictional inventories and local analysis exercises.
